# Applytics

## What is Applytics?

Applytics is an AI-powered job search and career operating system built for students, fresh graduates, and early-career professionals.

The goal of Applytics is simple:

Help people discover the right opportunities, prepare stronger applications, apply efficiently, and manage their entire job search journey from a single platform.

Today, job searching is fragmented.

Opportunities are scattered across LinkedIn, company career pages, Facebook groups, WhatsApp communities, job boards, university portals, and referral networks.

Candidates spend hours searching, applying, editing resumes, writing cover letters, and tracking applications manually.

Applytics aims to become the central hub where the entire job search process happens.

Instead of users jumping between 10 different platforms, Applytics becomes their career dashboard.

---

# The problem

## Problem 1: Job opportunities are scattered

Students often miss opportunities because jobs are posted across multiple channels.

A single company may post:

* LinkedIn
* Company Website
* Facebook
* WhatsApp Communities
* Discord Communities
* University Career Portals

Most candidates never see every opportunity available to them.

---

## Problem 2: Applications are inefficient

Most candidates:

* Use the same resume everywhere
* Apply without understanding requirements
* Spend 20-30 minutes per application
* Forget where they applied
* Miss deadlines
* Miss follow-ups

This creates a low-quality application process.

---

## Problem 3: Candidates don't know where they stand

Most job seekers have no visibility into:

* Application status
* Interview progress
* Response rates
* Resume performance
* Skill gaps

The process becomes random and frustrating.

---

# Vision

Applytics is not a job board.

Applytics is a career operating system.

The long-term vision is to become the platform that manages the complete career growth journey of a user.

A user should be able to:

* Discover opportunities
* Prepare applications
* Apply
* Track progress
* Improve skills
* Build professional profiles
* Prepare for interviews
* Get hired

All inside one system.

# Core product pillars

The production version of Applytics is built around four core pillars.

## 1. Opportunity Discovery

Help users discover relevant opportunities.

Features:

* AI-powered job recommendations
* Internship discovery
* Entry-level job discovery
* Graduate program discovery
* Remote jobs
* Freelance opportunities
* Scholarship opportunities
* Startup opportunities
* Aggregated jobs from multiple sources
* Personalized recommendation engine
* Location-based filtering
* Skill-based filtering
* Salary filtering
* Saved jobs

The system continuously learns from user behavior.

---

## 2. Application Engine

Help users apply faster and smarter.

Features:

### AI Resume Tailoring

Users maintain one master resume.

Applytics automatically creates role-specific versions.

The system:

* Matches job requirements
* Reorders experiences
* Recommends missing skills
* Optimizes keywords
* Improves ATS compatibility

### AI Cover Letter Generator

Generate personalized cover letters based on:

* Resume
* Job Description
* Company Information

### Quick Apply

Reduce application time from 20-30 minutes to a few minutes.

### Application Autofill

Automatically populate application forms.

### Application Templates

Reusable application assets.

---

# Product Philosophy

Job searching should not feel like a full-time job.

The current process is fragmented, repetitive, and inefficient.

Applytics exists to remove unnecessary friction from the job search journey and help people focus on getting hired rather than managing spreadsheets, documents, and repetitive tasks.

---

# Long-Term Goal

Become the default career platform for students and early-career professionals.

When someone starts searching for opportunities, preparing applications, improving skills, or planning their next career move, Applytics should be the first place they go.
